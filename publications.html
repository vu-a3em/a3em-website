Publications & Dissemination

Papers, datasets, and talks documenting the A3EM platform and its results.

Journal Article

System paper

Animal-Borne Adaptive Acoustic Monitoring

Jean, D., Wittemyer, G., Hedgecock, W., Turner, J., Lédeczi, Á., & Kalmár, G. (2025). Journal of Sensor and Actuator Networks, 14(4), 1–25.

This foundational system paper documents the sensor hardware, configurable firmware, unsupervised VAE-based adaptive filter, and the project's initial field deployments.

doi.org/10.3390/jsan14040066 ↗
In Progress

Forthcoming datasets & presentations

Elephant vocalization dataset

A first annotated dataset of elephant vocalizations — over 1,000 manually verified calls — is in preparation for public release with a citable DOI.

African Bioacoustics Community Conference 2026

An accepted conference presentation on the project's field results is scheduled for the 2026 African Bioacoustics Community Conference.

WildLabs tech talk

The team was invited to give a tech talk on the project at WildLabs, a global conservation-technology network of more than 10,000 members across 120 countries.
